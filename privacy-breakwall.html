1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Copiasoft (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when users access or play the mobile game “우당탕탕 벽뿌셔” (the App).

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, address, or email. The App may collect the following non-personal and service-related data:

  • Gameplay Data: stage progress, destroyed walls count, score, in-game currency, items.
  • Purchase Information: product ID, purchase status, purchase tokens from Google Play Billing (no credit card data is stored).
  • Device & Technical Data: device model, OS version, app version, country/language settings, crash logs.
  • Advertising Identifier (ADID) for ad personalization or measurement (depending on device settings).

3. How We Use the Information

  • Providing and maintaining the game service
  • Saving progress and managing cloud data
  • Processing and verifying in-app purchases

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services, which may collect data under their own policies:

Gameplay and purchase data are stored as long as required to operate the service. If a user requests deletion, the data will be removed unless retention is required by law.
